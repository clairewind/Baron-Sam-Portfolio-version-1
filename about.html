About Me

Dr. B Baron Sam



Dr. Baron Sam was born in Kanyakumari, India, in 1988. He received his Bachelors degree in B.Tech Information Technology from Anna University Chennai.His Masters Degree in M.E Computer Science from Veltech Technical university, Chennai,Master of Business Administration in Human Resource Management from Annamalai University, Chidambaram and Ph.D degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Sathyabama University, Chennai, India.Currently he is working as an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Ponjesly College of Engineering, Nagercoil, India. His-current research interests include Image Processing, Internet of things and Artificial Intelligence.

From Dr. Baron Sam


Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! I'm Dr. B Baron Sam, an Associate Professor and Assistant Head of Department with a passion for education and a dedication to nurturing the next generation of professionals.

With over 13 years of experience in higher education, I've cultivated a diverse skill set that includes expertise in programming, debugging, and lecturing. Armed with a PhD, I'm passionate about leveraging my skills to drive impactful solutions and deliver tangible results.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work across various institutions, including education and in-plant training, where I've honed my abilities in fostering collaboration and advancing technological innovation as an IEEE Coordinator. From spearheading key projects and initiatives to optimizing processes, I thrive on tackling challenges head-on and exceeding expectations.

Outside of work, I find joy in engaging with electrical works, repairing bikes, and tinkering with electronics. These hands-on activities provide me with a creative outlet and a sense of accomplishment outside of the classroom.

Looking ahead, my aspiration is to become a Principal, where I can leverage my leadership skills and passion for education to drive positive change and foster a culture of excellence. I am driven by the opportunity to make a lasting impact on the educational landscape.

In my quest for personal and professional growth, I am eager to engage in a dynamic and challenging environment where I can continue to evolve as an educator and contribute meaningfully to the field of higher education.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. I invite you to explore my portfolio further and connect with me to discuss potential collaborations and opportunities for growth.

Warm regards,
Dr. B Baron Sam
